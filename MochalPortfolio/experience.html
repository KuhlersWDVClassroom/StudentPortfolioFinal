Link Crew ( 2018 - Present )

During my Junior and Senior year of high school, I participated in a club called Link Crew, and what we do is help newer students and organize events for them. During the summer, we would help make posters, organize nametags, plan out how orientation would go, and called some of the incoming freshmen to let them know about orientation. We would take the students around the building and give them a tour while also making them feel welcome by playing a few games such as the name game. It helped them make a few new friends to start high school with and really impacted how they felt about the transition from middle to high school. Another thing we do during the year is give presentations to the freshmen about different subjects such as certain school policies and PSAs. We also walked them through MVP training.
